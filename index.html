Profile简介

Yunbo云波

I'm Yunbo — a maker and researcher from Northwest China working across games, real-time 3D and interactive media. I build VR worlds, playable systems and cross-media projects that turn hard questions — alienation, mental health, cultural identity, queer life — into spaces and systems you can move through.我是云波——来自中国西北的创作者与研究者，工作横跨游戏、实时三维与交互媒介。我做 VR 世界、可玩的系统和跨媒介项目，把那些不好回答的问题——异化、心理健康、文化身份、酷儿生活——转化成可以走进、可以操作的空间与系统。

I studied BA (Hons) Arts, Technology and Entertainment with Contemporary Entrepreneurialism at XJTLU, and I'm now an MA student in Digital Media: Production at UCL. I work mostly in Unreal Engine, Unity, Blender, Maya and TouchDesigner, and I care about making technology hold feeling.本科毕业于西交利物浦大学（XJTLU）艺术、科技与娱乐（含当代创业）专业，现于伦敦大学学院（UCL）攻读数字媒体硕士·制作方向。我主要用 Unreal Engine、Unity、Blender、Maya 和 TouchDesigner 创作，在意让技术承载情感。
